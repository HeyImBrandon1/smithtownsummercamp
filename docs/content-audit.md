## PAGE 10: FAQ (/frequently-asked-questions/)

### Hero
- Heading: "Frequently Asked Questions"

### Questions & Answers

**Q: How long does my child need to commit to camp?**
A: "The minimum amount of time that you need to register your child is for 1 week of fun!"

**Q: Do you offer any discounts?**
A: "We offer both an early bird discount and a multi week discount."

**Q: Do you have payment plans?**
A: "Yes. The first payment is due upon registration and the following are the 1st of every month."

**Q: What are the hours of camp?**
A: "Our camp day begins at 9:00 AM and goes until 4:00 PM! That's right, an entire day of fun! We also provide flexible before care and after care."

**Q: Do we have transportation?**
A: "Campers will be dropped off and picked up by their parents/guardians at Knox School 541 Long Beach Rd, St James, NY 11780. Flexible early drop-off and late pick-up will be available by special request."

**Q: Do you offer before and after Care?**
A: "Yes! We offer a flexible before care that's as early as 8 AM and an after care program as late as 6 PM for a small fee."

**Q: What happens in the event of weather?**
A: "Camp will always run! With extensive indoor space throughout the entire campus, camp is open rain or shine!"

**Q: Do we offer Lunch?**
A: "Yes! A professional caterer provides us with lunch and snacks daily. A weekly camp menu will be sent out prior to the start of camp."

**Q: Are we nut-free?**
A: "Yes. And we ask that if you participate in bringing your own lunch and and snack that you be respectfull of our 'nut-free' atmosphere."

**Q: Can I bring my own lunch?**
A: "Yes! As long as the lunch is 'nut-free'. We recommend a nutritious sandwich and healthy snacks. No refrigeration is available for packed lunches."

**Q: What should we pack?**
A: "All campers should arrive with a backpack that includes a water bottle, sunscreen, towel and one of their favorite nut free snacks."

**Q: What should we wear to camp?**
A: "All campers should be ready to play when they arrive. Please drop your campers off with sunscreen applied, athletic apparel and athletic footwear."
